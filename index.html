Projects

Faster is a fast casual game which makes the use of the public transport more intresting and fun. It even integrates with them, in order for the gamer to gain badges and power-ups that helps him on the game. The main aim of the game is to encourage the player to use more often the means of public transport by making them having fun in these.

The game is open source, licenced under Apache 2 licence.
